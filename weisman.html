This is a redesigned mural project for Weisman Children's Pediatric. With the idea of the treasure map, this design wants to motivate the patients here. Their journey at Weisman Children's Pediatric is like finding a treasure. "Treasure" here is their emotion, their spirit, their strength and their joy. These two murals are located in the gym and the welcome hallway.
